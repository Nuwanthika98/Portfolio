Awakening of the Artistic Non-Humans

Art has been a part of human culture for many centuries. Due to the evolution of technology, in recent years, there have been advances in AI, and people have been exploring its possible applications in various domains, including art. Various artistic creations like images, music & sound, dance and stories can be generated using AI.

Text- to-image generation is a field of AI research that aims to create images from natural language descriptions. It involves machine learning techniques to generate images that match a given textual description. Such models began to be developed in mid-2010s, as a result of advances in deep neural networks. This field has made significant progress in recent years, with many research groups and companies exploring new techniques and applications.

MidJourney, OpenAI’s DALL-E 2 and StabilityAI’s Stable Diffusion are some popular examples of text-to-image generation AI models, which create images of scenes, objects, and animals based on natural language descriptions. These models can generate images of things that don’t exist in the real world. As for the use of these AI generated images, it can be used in various fields such as advertising, design, gaming and entertainment.

NFT(Non-Fungible Tokens)s for AI generated images have gained popularity as a way for artists and creators to monetize their work and ensure that it is protected and preserved as a unique and valuable digital asset. As NFT is a unique digital certificate of ownership, stored on a blockchain, it allows an image to be traded and bought as a one-of-a-kind digital asset. The ownership of an NFT for an AI generated image can be transferred from one person to another, and its authenticity can be verified through the use of blockchain technology.

However, it is important to note that AI-generated images, like any digital asset, can be subject to copyright laws. So, it’s important to ensure that you have the right to use, distribute and sell the image, or obtain the necessary permissions or licenses before doing so. For example, according to the terms and conditions of DALL-E 2 and Stable Diffusion, you can use generated images, for any legal purpose, including for commercial use. On the other hand, MidJourney allows only paid members to use generated images commercially, while non-paid members can use them for non-commercial purposes only.

Finally, Text-to-image generation is a rapidly growing field with a lot of potential applications. As the technology continues to improve, we can expect to see more advanced and sophisticated AI models that can create more realistic images, and new use cases emerging.
